From the Principal's desk

K.R. Mangalam has always nurtured it's student's creative genius and hone their overall development. The brand name has been synonymous with quality education and is a pioneer in mooring the all round development of the students. Contributing to this, the school has launched its new IT club, SWAT whose mission is to promote knowledge of information technology through inter school events, workshops and seminars. The IT club will expand and enrich knowledge among not only its members but also to all the students who are interested in innovations in technology. To celebrate this new addition of ours, we proudly offer our young technodexterous, a technological extravaganza of event ‘COMP D’ AFFAIRS’ which endeavours to give the fillip and the boost to the hidden talents. Through this platform, we strive to equip the learners with the relevant skills and competencies. Henceforth, ‘COMP D’ AFFAIRS’ offers a chance to our technodexterous learners. May the best school win! We look forward to your school's participation in COMP D’ AFFAIRS 2017.

Rules

  • The participating teams should be accompanied by one teacher only.
  • Registration to be done on our website www.krmitclub.com
  • To qualify for the best school trophy, the school must participate in all events.
  • The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
  • Reporting time is 8.00 am. It is compulsory for all the participants to carry their school identity cards.

EVENTS

Photography

Photography is the art of observation having a little to do with what you see and a lot with what you think. Participants shall be asked to click photographs and edit it.

Date: 21 November 2017
Classes: IX-XII

Crossword

An exciting test of intelligence where participants have to solve the given puzzle with the help of provided clues leading to correct answers.The qualified teams will move to the Final Round.

Date: 21 November 2017
Classes: IX-XII

Shout Cast

Participants shall be asked to send entries online and the selected few shall appear for the final round. An exhilarating event based on editing and creativity.

Date: 21 November 2017
Classes: IX-XII

Cartoon Mania

The participants will be asked to design any fictional character.

Date: 21 November 2017
Classes: IV-V

Word-A-Ment

A stirring event based on calligram,the way of explaining words through pictures.The participants shall be given a word or a piece of text using which they shall be asked to design a layout of letters or images that creates a visual image related to the meaning of the words .

Date: 21 November 2017
Classes: IX-XII

Web Edge

For Prelims , Participants have to submit a website online in zip format. In finals , the topic will be declared on the spot. Participants will have to design a website using HTML , CSS , JAVASCRIPT Participants will be provided Sublime Text 3 , Google Chrome , Adobe Photoshop.

Date: 21 November 2017
Classes: IX-XII

Quiz

The ultimate test of knowledge that will consist of two rounds- Preliminary Round and the Final Round . Expect anything from the world of IT.

Date: 21 November 2017
Classes: IX-XII

Pwned!

Games will be declared on the spot. Participants will be provided with PS4. Participants can bring any external hardware except storage devices.
