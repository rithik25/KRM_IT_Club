About

S.W.A.T.

The IT club of K.R.Mangalam World School G.K.2 comprises of the most fervent,intellectual and inventive students of the Computer Science section of the school. These specially chosen students have worked hard throughout the year in order to make COMP D’AFFAIRE a huge success.
COMP D'AFFAIRE

COMP D’AFFAIRE is a two day event-16 to 17 November 2017. The competition consists of 7 events to check the skills and wit of the participants. The events shall be marked by leading computer wizards from the IT sector.

Rules

  • Contestants can take part in non-clashing events and there will be no adjustment in the timings for the event.
  • All the members of the team should be present at the time of start of the event.
  • Any changes in the registered participants must be noted at the time of registration.
  • The last date of registration for the competition is __
  • The teams must report at the registration desk latest by 9 a.m.
  • Rules regarding each event must be followed.
  • The decision of the judges will be imperative and final.
